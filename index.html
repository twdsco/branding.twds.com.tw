Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use the appropriate logo variation for your background
  • Maintain the logo's aspect ratio when resizing
  • Ensure adequate spacing around the logo (minimum 1x logo height)
  • Use vector formats (SVG) for digital applications when possible

Do not

  • Don't stretch or distort the logo
  • Don't change the logo colors
  • Don't place the logo on busy backgrounds
  • Don't use low-resolution versions for print
